## Inclining Experiment — Box Catamaran

**SEF427 Naval Architecture** · AutoCAD, Excel, Lab Testing

Transverse stability investigation on a box-shaped catamaran model. The experiment measures metacentric height (GM) through the inclining method across two conditions: solid ballast and free surface effect.

![[catamaran-heel-test.png]]

The model at 0° and 5° heel — showing the pendulum deflection used to measure the angle of inclination and calculate GM.

![[catamaran-experiment-drawing.png]]

General arrangement drawing showing Part A (solid ballast) and Part B (solid + water ballast) configurations, with front elevation, side elevation, and plan views.

| Metric | Theoretical | Experimental | Discrepancy |
|---|---|---|---|
| GM (solid ballast) | 0.2169 m | 0.2340 m | 7.9% |
| GM (free surface) | 0.2114 m | 0.2645 m | 25.1% |
| FSE correction | 0.00794 m | — | — |
